Oreo Balls

Description

Nothing like a quick chocolate delight dessert to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 36 Oreos
  • 1 block of Cream Cheese
  • Chocolate almond bark

Steps

  1. Prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Use a food processor to crush the whole pack of oreos into crumbs.
  3. Mix Oreos with cream cheese until it is well blende-d.
  4. Shape Oreo mixture into a 1 inch ball, then align on the baking sheet.
  5. Chill the Oreo balls in the freezer for 15 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, crush 3 Oreos to crumbs and set aside.
  7. Melt almond bark according to instructions on the package.
  8. Dip Oreo balls into the melted almond bark using a spoon.
  9. Return Oreo balls to the baking sheet and sprinkle with crushed Oreo before the chocolate sets.
