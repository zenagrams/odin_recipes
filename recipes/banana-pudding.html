Banana Pudding

Description

No Bake banana pudding is the perfect dessert to satisfy crunchy, sweet and creamy cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 package instant vanilla pudding mix (5 ounce)
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed.
  • 1 (16 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat pudding mix and milk for 2 minutes.
  2. Blend condensed milk until smooth.
  3. Stir in vanilla and fold in whipped topping.
  4. Layer wafers, bananas, and pudding mixture in serving containers.
  5. Chill until serving.
