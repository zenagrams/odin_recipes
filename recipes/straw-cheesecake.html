Strawberry Cheesecake

Description

Need a quick, no bake recipe for a potluck? Whip up this beautiful strawberry cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package strawberry Jello
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (5 ounce) can cold evaporated milk
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Steps

  1. Dissolve Jello in boiling water, cool in refrigerator until thick, but not set, 20 minutes.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat evaporated milk in a separate bowl with an electric mixer until whipped and thick. Gradually pour strawberry gelatin mixture into evaporated milk, beating constantly. Fold cream cheese mixture into Jello-milk mixture.
  4. Pour cheesecake filling into the crust.
  5. Refrigerate until cake is set at least 3.5 hours.
